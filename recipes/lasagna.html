Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients:

  • Italian Sausage
  • Onion and Garlic
  • 1 Can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Cans tomato sauce
  • 2 Cans tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Cooking Directions

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
